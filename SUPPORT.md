## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I change the app language?**
Go to **iOS Settings → Vivo - Bodyweight and Running → Language**. The app relaunches automatically to apply the change. Supported languages: English, German.

**How do I delete my workout, exercise, or run history?**
Individual sessions and notes can be deleted from inside the app. To remove all data at once, delete the app and reinstall it — since everything is stored on-device, this permanently erases it.

**Why does the app ask for location access?**
Location is only used for the run tracking feature, to record your route, distance, and pace. Location data never leaves your device. See the [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.html) for details.

**The app crashed or isn't working correctly — what should I do?**
Try restarting the app first. If the problem continues, please email us with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

**How do I request a feature or report a bug?**
We'd love to hear from you — send us an email using the contact details below.
