# Privacy Policy — Vivo - Bodyweight and Running

**Last updated: July 12, 2026**

Vivo is a fitness app for iPhone. This policy explains how your data is handled.

---

## Summary

Vivo is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate a backend server, do not require an account, and do not collect personal data about you.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is saved locally on your iPhone and never transmitted anywhere:

- **Display name** — the name you enter during onboarding
- **Body weight** — used only to estimate calorie burn; stored in your device's local preferences
- **Unit preferences** — metric or imperial (weight, distance)
- **Workout & exercise history** — completed workouts, exercises, sets, reps, duration, estimated calories, notes, and perceived difficulty ratings
- **Run history** — date, distance, pace, duration, estimated calories, and GPS route coordinates
- **GPS route data** — latitude/longitude coordinates recorded during runs, stored on-device only

All data is stored using SwiftData, Apple's on-device database framework. Deleting the app permanently erases all of this data.

---

## Location & GPS

The run tracking feature uses your iPhone's GPS to record your route, distance, and pace.

- Location access is requested only when you start the app ("When In Use").
- Background location is used while a run is in progress so tracking continues when the screen is off.
- GPS coordinates are stored on your device only and are never transmitted to any server or third party.
- You can revoke location permission at any time in **iOS Settings → Vivo → Location**.

---

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.
- We do not require a network connection for any core feature.
- We do not share your data with any third party — because we never have it.
- We do not use Apple's HealthKit framework.
- We do not collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or IP addresses.

---

## Third-Party Libraries

Vivo uses one third-party library:

| Library | Purpose | Data collected |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| [Lottie](https://github.com/airbnb/lottie-ios) | Renders animations locally on-device | None |

Lottie makes no network requests and collects no data.

---

## Your Control

- **Change language** — iOS Settings → Vivo - Bodyweight and Running → Language (the OS relaunches the app to apply the change)
- **Delete individual sessions** — from inside the app
- **Delete all data** — delete and reinstall the app

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this approach changes — for example, if we ever add an optional account or cloud sync feature — we will update this document and notify users before any data leaves the device.
